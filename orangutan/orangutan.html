About

Human activity such as illegal logging, mining, and road construction in the tropical rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra are risking the lives of orangutans everywhere and overall risking extintion for the species. At a rate of 2,000 - 3,000 of these monkeys being killed per year, there is a total of about 104,700 orangutans left on our planet. This sweet species has lost over 80% of their habitat in the past two decades, also losing a third of their population in that time frame.

How to Help

There are many ways to help orangutans, but the best way to make a change is by donating money to facilities that care for these special animals. Check out the International Animal Rescue to 'adopt' an orangutan of your choice and save a life today.
